My Life Story

I said I was from Hershey, but I was actually born in Saigon, Vietnam. I moved to the States when I was just two years old.
Growing up, I was raised by my Vietnamese mom and an American step-dad. Due to this, my household was very multicultural.
I often had a mixture of Vietnamese and American food for meals, or whole days dedicated to one cuisine. Besides that, I grew
up speaking both Vietnamese and English, so I got a taste of different languages from an early age. This is actually why my POE
is in French and Spanish. I really enjoyed the idea of the multiculturalism I was exposed to, especially the idea of learning different
languages. I can proudly say that I can actually hold a conversation in 4 different languages. However, I am also using Duolingo to
learn other languages such as Portuguese, Italian, and even Latin, so I am always learning something new.

My Present Journey

I am often asked by everybody, whether it is friends or family about what I hope to achieve with my POE, and honestly,
I am not 100% sure myself. I have thought of many different professions, and that can be seen with the constant changes
I make with my POE. I officially came in my first year wanting to study French and International Politics. That quickly
faded away as I took a variety of classes to get a taste of everything. I've thought of adding anything from PACS to Geology
to History. I originally had taken both a French and a Spanish class the fall semester of my first year because I was proficient
at both. I decided to stop Spanish after that because I was not sure if it would be helpful to me in the long run regarding a future
career. However, after talking with many people, and a lot of thinking on my own part, I realized that there would be many job
opportunities available, especially since I know 4 languages.
