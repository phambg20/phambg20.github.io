The Basics

My name is Buu Pham, and I am a junior here at Juniata College. I study French and Spanish with a Secondary POE in Anthropology.
I hail from Hershey, Pennsylvania, and that is who I am simplified.

But WHO am I really?

WHO? WHAT? WHEN? WHERE? HOW?

I said I was from Hershey, but I was actually born in Saigon, Vietnam. I moved to the States when I was just two years old.
Growing up, I was raised by my Vietnamese mom and an American step-dad. Due to this, my household was very multicultural.
I often had a mixture of Vietnamese and American food for meals, or whole days dedicated to one cuisine. Besides that, I grew
up speaking both Vietnamese and English, so I got a taste of different languages from an early age. This is actually why my POE
is in French and Spanish. I really enjoyed the idea of the multiculturalism I was exposed to, especially the idea of learning different
languages. I can proudly say that I can actually hold a conversation in 4 different languages. However, I am also using Duolingo to
learn other languages such as Portuguese, Italian, and even Latin, so I am always learning something new.
