Who We Are


With 20,000 students and even more alumni, the Chocolatetown family is vast and found all over the world.
Our way of thinking stems from the same thoughts of Milton Hershey when he chose Derry Township as the setting for his factory, freshness.
While he was pursuing fresh milk for his chocolate, we are pursuing fresh ideas for and from our students. Family is a core value here at CU, so
cooperation and working together is a key part of our success. We are constantly working with his company today to provide factory experience
and exciting opportunities and internships not just to our business students but beyond.
