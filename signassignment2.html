Who We Are


With over 20,000 students and even more alumni, the Chocolatetown family is abundant and found all over the world.
Our way of thinking stems from the same thoughts of Milton Hershey when he chose Derry Township as the setting for his factory, freshness.
While he was pursuing fresh milk for his chocolate, we are pursuing fresh ideas for and from our students. Family is a core value here at CU,
so cooperation and working together is a key part of our success. We are constantly working with his company and others to provide factory
experience and exciting opportunities and internships not just to our business students but beyond. On campus, we have over 200 acres of land
with state-of-the-art facilities and expert faculty working inside of them. Our diverse student body works to tackle challenging coursework
and problems facing the modern world with faculty advisors standing beside them the entire time. Besides from academic support,
our students are also provided with a lively campus life with our sports as well as our countless number of clubs harboring
many different interests. Milton Hershey strived to provide academic and financial support for all of the students of his school.
We are achieving the same goal and more to carry on his message, and to make sure that our students create their own legacy just as he did.
