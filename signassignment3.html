"Don't think about who you are now, think about who you want to become."

Here at Chocolatetown University, We offer a wide variety of degrees in order to create a balanced liberal arts education
and to accommodate for the wide variety of interests of our students. We have over a hundred undergraduate majors and
minors, as well as our Master's and Doctorate programs.

Our programs cover all fields from STEM to the liberal arts, to business and law.
If you don't know what you want to study quite yet, that is completely fine. You can come in as an undecided major.
Have more than one area that peaks your interest? Our vast selection makes sure you can do a double major or minor
at no extra cost.

The internships and opportunities along with the 4-year education we provide our students gives them the proper foundation
and skills to succeed in their field of study in the professional world.

Let us help you in beginning your journey to start the rest of your life.
